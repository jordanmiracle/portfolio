Some things about me

I’m a web and software developer with a love for projects that make meaningful differences. Many developers fall into a few categories. Some see the advantage in having a skill that can earn them a lot of money, or want to build a popular app. Others love designing because they have an artistic eye. Others want to change the way that communication and the internet work altogether.

Personally, I use what I know to help people who are pushing for large, structural change in the world. Whether it be the climate crisis, sustainable farming, social equity, or any other number of things. I learned pretty quickly that my best work happened when my heart was in the project.

I am a voracious seeker, although I don’t always know what of. This is why programming, and technology in general is so wonderful. It develops so fast, that one doesn’t have the chance to stagnate.

Currently, I am working as a freelance developer. Anything involving web development I can pick up pretty quickly, but I also love language procesing and data science. If you are interested in working together on a project, or need to hire somebody, feel free to contact me. I’m always willing to listen to ideas.
